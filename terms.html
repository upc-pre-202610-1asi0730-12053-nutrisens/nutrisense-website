Terms and Conditions

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Welcome to NutriSense, a SaaS web platform designed to help individuals reach their fitness goals (weight loss or muscle gain) through intelligent nutritional tracking and personalized, context-aware recommendations. By accessing our web application, you agree to the following terms.

1. Service Description

NutriSense is a smart nutritional tracking platform that offers:

  • Goal Tracking: Logging of weight and height, including calculations for metrics such as BMI, BMR, and TDEE.
  • Nutrition Log: Manual food search and meal logging categorized by time of day.
  • Smart Scan: Visual analysis of meals and restaurant menus through photography.
  • Contextual Recommendations: Suggestions based on current weather (via OpenWeatherMap) and geographic location (Travel Mode).
  • Activity Sync: Integration with Google Fit API to import steps and active calories burned.

2. Medical and Ethical Responsibility

Not a Professional Substitute. NutriSense provides personalized recommendations based on real-world context, but does not substitute professional medical advice. Users with medical conditions are encouraged to consult a qualified healthcare provider.

Manual Confirmation of Visual Analysis. Results from the Smart Scan feature (photo analysis) must be manually confirmed by the user before being saved to their log, in order to maintain nutritional accuracy.

Dietary Restrictions. While the platform applies filters for allergies, intolerances, and medical conditions, the final responsibility for food consumption lies with the user.

ACM/IEEE Ethical Principles. NutriSense is developed following the ethical guidelines of the ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct and the IEEE Code of Ethics, which include: acting in the public interest, being honest and transparent, avoiding harm, protecting user privacy, and ensuring accessibility. These principles guide every product decision.

CIP Ethical Commitment (Código de Ética del CIP, Peru). The engineering team adheres to the professional code of ethics of the Colegio de Ingenieros del Perú (CIP), prioritizing the safety, well-being, and trust of the general public in all technical decisions.

3. Plans and Billing

The service is structured into three mandatory subscription tiers:

  • Basic Plan ($7.99/month): Includes manual food logging and a basic dashboard.
  • Pro Plan ($14.99/month): Includes all Basic features, plus Smart Scan, Travel Mode, weather-based recommendations, and pantry management.
  • Premium Plan ($19.99/month): Includes all Pro features, plus wearable synchronization, unlimited history, and PDF reports.

Subscriptions are billed monthly. Users may upgrade or cancel their plan at any time. No refunds are issued for partial billing periods unless required by applicable law.

4. Privacy and Technology

Third-Party API Usage. NutriSense uses external services including Google Cloud Vision, Open Food Facts, USDA FoodData Central, OpenWeatherMap, and Google Fit API to process nutritional information and images. Data shared with these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Geolocation. The use of the Browser Geolocation API is required for the Travel Mode and weather recommendation features. Location data is used solely for contextual recommendations and is not stored beyond the active session without explicit user consent.

Accessibility and Language. The platform complies with web accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 / ARIA) and is available in English (en_US) and Latin-American Spanish (es_419).

Data Security. User data is protected using industry-standard encryption practices. NutriSense does not sell personal data to third parties. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time by contacting support.

5. Service Limitations

NutriSense does not include e-commerce integrations, real-time delivery analysis (e.g., Rappi, Uber Eats), or full voice assistant functionality in its standard version. These features are outside the current scope and may be evaluated in future versions.

The Smart Scan feature provides nutritional estimates based on visual recognition. Accuracy depends on image quality and food variety coverage in the nutritional database. Users are responsible for verifying and adjusting logged values.

6. Changes to These Terms

NutriSense reserves the right to update these Terms and Conditions at any time. Users will be notified of material changes via the platform or by email. Continued use of the service after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

For questions or concerns regarding these terms, please contact us at legal@nutrisense.app or through our Contact page.

  • Sincronización de actividad: Integración con la API de Google Fit para importar pasos y calorías activas quemadas.
